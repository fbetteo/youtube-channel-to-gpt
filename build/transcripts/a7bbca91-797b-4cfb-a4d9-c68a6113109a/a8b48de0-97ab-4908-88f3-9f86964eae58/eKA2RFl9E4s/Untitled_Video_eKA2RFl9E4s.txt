Did you know that some old Japanese swords were so long they had to be drawn in two moves? These giant blades, called odachi, could reach up to 2 m and were used in feudal Japan. Thanks to their long reach, they were used by cavalry units to break through enemy lines on the battlefield. But due to their size and weight, they were hard to master and rarely used in real combat.
