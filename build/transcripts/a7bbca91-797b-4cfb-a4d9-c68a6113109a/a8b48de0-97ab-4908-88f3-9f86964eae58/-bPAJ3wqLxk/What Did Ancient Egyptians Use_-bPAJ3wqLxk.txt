Video Title: What Did Ancient Egyptians Use as a Fridge
View Count: 1,773,580

How did ancient people keep things cold without electricity for thousands of years? This was one of the first refrigerators dating back to the Egyptians. Two clay pots were used, a smaller one placed inside a larger one. The gap between them was filled with sand, then soaked with water. A wet cloth was placed on top. As the water evaporated, it drew heat away from the outer pot, cooling the inside. This evaporative cooling process kept food fresh much longer, even in the desert
