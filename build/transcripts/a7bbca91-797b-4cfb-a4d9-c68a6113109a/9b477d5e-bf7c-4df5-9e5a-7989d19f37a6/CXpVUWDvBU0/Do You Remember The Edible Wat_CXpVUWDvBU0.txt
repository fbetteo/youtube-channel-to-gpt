do you remember the edible water bottle when it first came out with its edible outer membrane made of natural seaweed and biodegradable structure it aimed to eliminate the need for plastic bottles and cups while leaving no waste in nature it was also quite fun to consume with its membrane structure that melts in the mouth but it did not become widespread due to its production cost fragile structure and hygienic storage difficulties
