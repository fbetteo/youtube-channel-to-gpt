Video Title: Ancient Knives That Shaped Wood by Hand
View Count: 19,005,545

Before modern technology, these ingenious ancient knives were used to shape wood by hand. Draw knives made it possible to shape curved structures like canoes. But the process required great skill and physical effort. Even simple items like spoons were made through long detailed carving using specially curved blades. Though very old, this traditional method is still kept alive today, especially as a woodworking hobby.
