this antique 18th century mechanical vacuum cleaner was used before electricity it required considerable effort to remove dust and debris using a manually operated blower system and a piston to create suction the user pumped a lever to generate air flow which compressed the blower and sucked in dust and debris the gradual introduction of electrical equipment eliminated this problem
