in the 19th century gas lamps were widely used to illuminate streets the responsibility of lighting them each evening and extinguishing them in the morning fell to lamp lighters using long sticks or ladders they would ignite the lamps ensuring that cities remained bright throughout the night lamp lighting was once a crucial profession playing a key role in both safety and Aesthetics this traditional practice is still preserved in some cities as a nostalgic activity
