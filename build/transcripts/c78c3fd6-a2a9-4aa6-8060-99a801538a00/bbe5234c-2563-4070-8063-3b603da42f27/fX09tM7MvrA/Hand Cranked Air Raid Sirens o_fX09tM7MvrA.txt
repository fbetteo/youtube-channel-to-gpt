we are fortunate to have never heard this sound hand cranked Air Raid Sirens were portable warning devices used in the 1940s to alert the public of Air Raids by cranking the handle the rotor spins creating an air flow that passes through the stator's holes where it is rapidly interrupted to produce a vibrating Soundwave even a small hand cranked siren could reach over 120 DB making it an effective warning system even without electricity a rising and falling tone signaled an imminent attack while a steady tone indicated that the danger had passed
