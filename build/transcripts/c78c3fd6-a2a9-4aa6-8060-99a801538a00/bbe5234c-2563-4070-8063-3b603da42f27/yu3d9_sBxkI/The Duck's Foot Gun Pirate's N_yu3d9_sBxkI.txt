this fascinating weapon served an incredible purpose in the 18th century known as the Ducks foot gun it was a pirates nightmare with its multiple barrels angled in different directions this unique design allowed it to fire at multiple targets over a wide area making it ideal for Sailors and prison guards to control large groups however its simultaneous firing led to significant recoil and low accuracy eventually rendering it obsolete
