2,000 years ago, people used this ingenious system to lift heavy objects. The Archimedes windless is a mechanical device used for lifting heavy loads. It works by wrapping a rope around a stick. Another stick is used as a handle to turn the windless. As you rotate it, the rope tightens and pulls the load upward. Legend says Archimedes once pulled a massive ship alone to prove the windless's
