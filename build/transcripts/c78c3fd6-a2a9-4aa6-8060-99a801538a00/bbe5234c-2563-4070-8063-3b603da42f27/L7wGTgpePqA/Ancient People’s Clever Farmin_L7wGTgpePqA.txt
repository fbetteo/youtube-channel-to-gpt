How ancient people transformed farming and fishing using just two sticks. By tapping or vibrating wooden stakes, they mimicked the sound of rain, causing worms to rise to the surface to avoid drowning. If many worms appeared, it meant the soil was rich, moist, and perfect for farming. It also made bait collection for fishing incredibly
